About INCSTIC2026

Welcome to INCSTIC2026: 2nd International Conference on Smart Technologies and Intelligent Computing will be organized by School of Computer Science and Engineering, during 20th- 21st November 2026 at Geeta University, Campus – Panipat, Haryana, India.

The conference is an annual global event that brings together researchers, academicians, industry leaders, and technology enthusiasts from around the world. It serves as a dynamic platform for presenting and discussing groundbreaking innovations, cutting-edge research, and transformative ideas in the rapidly evolving fields of smart technologies and intelligent computing. With the increasing integration of intelligence into modern systems and the widespread proliferation of smart devices, INCSTIC aims to address both the challenges and opportunities arising from this technological revolution. The conference highlights the pivotal role of smart technologies and intelligent computing in shaping the future of industries, societies, and global economies.

INCSTIC fosters meaningful collaboration among researchers, academia, and industry, helping bridge the gap between theoretical research and real-world applications. Participants gain valuable insights into the latest advancements in areas such as smart cities, intelligent systems, advanced networking, and data analytics. As well, the conference serves as a catalyst for innovation by encouraging interdisciplinary research and the development of novel solutions to contemporary challenges, including sustainability, cybersecurity, and user-centric design.

Pedagogy

All accepted and presented papers will be included in the conference proceedings. Selected high-quality papers may be considered for submission to reputed journals or digital libraries, subject to their respective review and indexing policies.

The core objective of the conference is to bring together scholars, scientists, and industry professionals from across the globe onto a common platform to exchange knowledge, foster innovation, and encourage collaboration. The conference is guided by the following objectives:

  • To provide a platform for presenting cutting-edge research in the fields of smart technologies and intelligent computing.
  • To explore the integration of intelligent systems in diverse domains, including engineering, healthcare, education, and business.
  • To facilitate strong collaboration between academia and industry, enabling impactful and real-world innovations.
  • To promote interdisciplinary research and develop smart technological solutions to address pressing societal challenges.

Vision and Goals

To establish the International Conference on Smart Technologies and Intelligent Computing (InCSTIC) as a globally recognized platform for advancing knowledge, fostering innovation, and contributing to a smarter, more sustainable, and interconnected world.
The key goals of the conference include:

  • Advance Research and Innovation: Promote the presentation and exchange of groundbreaking research, emerging technologies, and innovative solutions in the fields of smart technologies and intelligent computing.
  • Encourage Global Collaboration: Bring together researchers, academicians, and industry experts from across the world to foster partnerships and collaborative research initiatives.
  • Bridge Academia and Industry: Facilitate meaningful interactions between academic researchers and industry professionals to translate theoretical advancements into practical, real-world applications.
  • Promote Interdisciplinary Approaches: Encourage cross-domain research that integrates multiple disciplines to solve complex technological and societal challenges.
  • Support Sustainable Development: Highlight the role of intelligent systems and smart technologies in addressing global challenges such as sustainability, energy efficiency, and smart infrastructure.
  • Nurture Emerging Talent: Provide a platform for young researchers, scholars, and students to showcase their work, gain feedback, and engage with experts in the field.

About Geeta University

Established on a foundation of educational excellence since 1985, Geeta University delivers a transformative learning experience that seamlessly integrates global knowledge with Indian values. Dedicated to shaping well-rounded professionals, the university focuses on cultivating innovation, ethics, and a strong commitment to societal progress. Its advanced curriculum, paired with a diverse and accomplished faculty, empowers students to realize their aspirations and unlock their full potential.

The campus fosters holistic development through international exchange programs and an academic environment that balances rigorous learning with real-world application. Guided by the philosophy of Karma and symbolized by colors representing knowledge and technological advancement, Geeta University is committed to nurturing future-ready leaders equipped to navigate and excel in an ever-evolving world. University is established as per section 2F of UGC Act, 1956 vide UGC Letter No. F.No.8-3/2021 (CPP-I/PU).

About GTH

Geeta Technical Hub (GTH) is the training division of Geeta University, dedicated to equipping students with industry-relevant skills. We offer a variety of certification programs, hands-on projects, internships, and placement opportunities that bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world corporate needs. Our programs cover multiple technologies, including Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Cloud Computing, Cybersecurity, and more. Students gain practical experience through live projects and internships with top-tier companies, preparing them for successful careers in the tech industry. In addition to technical expertise, GTH emphasizes soft skills like communication and problem-solving, ensuring well-rounded development. Our strong industry partnerships provide exclusive placement opportunities, helping students step confidently into the job market. At GTH, we’re committed to shaping the future of technology by fostering innovation, professionalism, and excellence in every student.
